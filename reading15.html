A loooooooong Way to Go

In this brief writing, I'd like to take stock in the current state of my website. I feel that a lof of this information is self-apparent and this is not going to be a long post, but I still feel it worth addressing. I am not currently happy with the way things are looking. While the blog posts are alright, the overall appearance of my UI is bland and repetitive, and the home screen is starting to become overloaded with a bunch of information. In these coming weeks, I plan to take a look at more portfolios and see what they are doing correctly and how I can improve. I also need to improve my general HTML, JavaScript, and CSS skills, as there are many ideas I have that I simply do not know how to implement yet, such as a searchbar or a unified size for blogpost icons.
